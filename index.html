Customer Problem Statement

Problems with parking have become universal and increasingly frustrating. Metropolitan centers in the U.S. are growing and the supply for parking is falling short. By introducing an organized valet service and integrating smart technologies, such as real-time tracking and mobile scheduling, we can significantly reduce the time spent searching for parking. ValetGo aims to solve the growing problem of parking with transparent communication, interactive straightforward user interface, and smooth running software.
